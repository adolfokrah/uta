Personal information is data that can be used to identify or contact a single person. You may be asked to provide your personal information before accessing some features of the UTA app. Uta does not share this personal information with anyone with this Privacy Policy. They may also combine it with other information to provide and improve our products, services, content, and advertising. You are not required to provide the personal information that we have requested, but, if you chose not to do so, in many cases you will not be able to explore all the features UTA has to offer. Here are some examples of the types of personal information Uta may collect and how we may use it:
What personal information we collect
When you create a Uta ID, identify songs, search for songs, download songs, connect to our services, contact us including by social media or support, we may collect a variety of information, including your name, email address and picture (only if there’s one on your Facebook or Google account).
We will never ask for a government issued ID, passwords or any other very sensitive information.
How we use your personal information
We may process your personal information: for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, with your consent, for compliance with a legal obligation to which UTA is subject, for the performance of a contract to which you are party, in order to protect your vital interests, or when we have assessed it is necessary for the purposes of the legitimate interests pursued by UTA or a third party to whom it may be necessary to disclose information.
The personal information we collect allows you to saved your identified songs and add songs to your library.If you don’t want to be on our mailing list, you can opt-out anytime by updating your preferences.
We also use personal information to help us create, develop, operate, deliver, and improve our services, content and advertising.
From time to time, we may use your personal information to send important notices, such as communications about changes to our terms, conditions, and policies. Because this information is important to your interaction with UTA, you may not opt out of receiving these communications.  We also use personal information to store your Utas, settings and preferences so that it can be accessed anywhere in the world as soon as you login to the app.
Protection of Personal Information
Uta only keeps personal information such as username and email address and picture provided by third party sources (Google, Facebook) and nothing else. Personal information collected are not visible to other users and cannot be read, collected, or used by them.
